Where in this wonderful nation did I live?

South Africa, one of the largest nations in Africa, was my home for 2 years. But where in the Rainbow Nation did I reside? The province of Gauteng would be the answer to that. This province has Johannesburg, the biggest city in the country and is the most developed province in the country. In Gauteng, I lived in Johannesburg, the largest city in South Africa. But going down deeper, where in Johannesburg? The answer to that is Sandton (nicknamed "Africa's Richest Square Mile"), the business and fianance hub of South Africa.
